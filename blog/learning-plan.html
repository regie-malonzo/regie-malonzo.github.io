I consider myself to be quite open-minded when it comes to learning. I'm always eager to explore new ideas and perspectives, which I believe enriches my understanding of various subjects. I want to become more confident in myself and my work at Dev Academy. I hope to overcome my fear of judgment and feel comfortable putting myself out there. This means being more confident in speaking up, sharing my ideas, and presenting my work without worrying too much about what others might think.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I'm committed to staying organized and on top of my workload throughout the program. If I ever feel overwhelmed or stressed, I won't hesitate to reach out for help from my facilitator or classmates. By staying organized, engaged, and willing to seek assistance when needed, I'm confident I can effectively manage my workload and succeed in the program

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

When I find myself unable to solve a problem after a reasonable amount of time and I notice I'm going on a tangent, I commit to seeking help in a timely manner. I will first attempt to solve the issue myself by researching and troubleshooting. If I still can't find a solution and realize I'm spending too much time on it, I will promptly reach out to my facilitator or post a question on the Discord channel under the #tech-questions section. This will ensure that I receive timely assistance and avoid wasting unnecessary time.
