EQ and IQ

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence or EQ its the ability to recognize, understand and manage you own emotions and the emotions of others. its made up of different characteristics and skill sets all of which can be learned.

How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is about understanding and managing your emotions and the emotions of others. It's about things like empathy, knowing how to handle stressful situations, and building strong relationships. where IQ measures how well you can think, reason, and solve problems. It's all about your cognitive abilities, like how fast you can learn new things or how well you understand complex ideas.

Why is EQ important?

EQ, or emotional intelligence, is important because it helps us:

  • Get along better with others by understanding their feelings.
  • Communicate effectively by expressing ourselves clearly and listening well.
  • Solve problems and resolve conflicts calmly and constructively.
  • Be better leaders by inspiring and motivating others.
  • Deal with change and stress more easily.
  • Be happier and more satisfied with life overall.

EQ makes life smoother and more enjoyable by improving how we understand and manage emotions, both ours and others'.
