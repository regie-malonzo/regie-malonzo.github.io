Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

Neuroplasticity


How does understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefit people?

That both physical and mental are link together, anything you do mentally can have the same outcome as a physical practice. The more you apply yourself in the process the better the outcome may be for you.


How might you engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your own benefit?

Being able to change my mindset that just because it's not working at the moment it doesn't mean I've failed. neuroplasticity allows your brain to learn something for failure/mistake. The more i practice and apply my process of understanding the better i am able to change that.


What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

some ways i can increase my neuroplasticity is by researching, practice and applying all of those into my learning and also the way i live.

Growth Mindset


Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

Once reading and looking into growth mindset and the power of "yet" i can go into this course with a different perspective. To go for the challenges about to be set, find the process of understanding.


In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

Yes i believe that it did surprise me because of the way i was brought up and thinking how it was always fail or succeed.


How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

To go into this course with a growth mindset by having an open mind about the process and ask for help. Trial and error, giving it my 100%
